# AI Guidelines

## Non-Negotiables
- Follow the load order and Plan/Act rules in `clinerules/00_index.md`.
- Use the workflow that matches the task and do not skip required steps.
- Produce required artifacts using templates in `ai/templates/`.
- Keep diffs minimal and avoid drive-by refactors.
- Record tests and results in the PR summary.
